Our Mission

CreateCrescendos is dedicated to furthering the music education of every student regardless of location, socioeconomic status, or experience through free, high quality, and personalized one-on-one mentorship. We recognize that during the pandemic, many students don't have access to private lesson teachers. Our mentors are hand-chosen high school juniors and seniors or college music students with accomplishments in their instruments. Through their personal experiences, they are able to offer genuine advice to help music students of all ages with any problem they may have.




Our Method

CreateCrescendos understands that there is wide range of music students with different ages, instruments, and questions. Therefore, our algorithm allows prospective mentees to select exactly what they are looking for - whether it be feedback on a solo, preparation for an audition, or general advice for improvement. With general information about your age, contact information, location, and instrument, our algorithm will match you with a list of mentors! From there, choose your mentor and preferred method of contact, then connect!
